The Challenge

1 Teacher. 100 Students.

In rural Malawi, overflowing classrooms make it impossible for teachers to answer every query. Students risk falling behind simply because there isn't enough individualized attention to go around.

The Solution

1 Tablet. 4 Students. Infinite Answers.

Chambo AI is optimized for shared hardware. Four students can cluster around one tablet, access their class materials, watch explanation videos, and ask the AI questions they couldn't ask in class.
